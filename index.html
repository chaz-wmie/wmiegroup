About WMIE

WMIE stands for Wholesale Management & Infrastructure Expansion. The purpose is simple: make difficult infrastructure projects easier to move, easier to explain, and easier to execute.

We support teams that need help pulling together engineering, insurance, bonding, grant writing, marketing, stakeholder relationships, and construction coordination.

We Make It Easy.
