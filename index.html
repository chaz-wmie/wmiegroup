Front page message

Strategy, relationships, and readiness for complex broadband opportunities.

Wholesale Management & Infrastructure Expansion (WMIE) is a strategic support firm focused on commercial insurance strategy, surety bonding, sales development, marketing alignment, partner engagement, and high-level corporate and tribal relations.

WMIE supports partners pursuing BEAD initiatives, USDA-funded projects, federal contract requirements, and regional expansion efforts by helping them communicate effectively, build meaningful relationships, and present strong, competitive proposals.

WMIE does not operate as a provider. It supports bandwidth-sales partnerships by connecting organizations with trusted bandwidth partners while partner companies handle service operations.

WMIE maintains a referral partnership with an industry-leading engineering firm, giving clients access to engineering expertise without WMIE performing engineering work.
